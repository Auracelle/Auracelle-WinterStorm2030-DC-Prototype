A governance capacity instrument, not a combat simulator. WinterStorm2030 tracks a live NATO Capability Gap as NATO/Nordic actors (Finland, Norway, Sweden, Denmark/Greenland, Iceland) and adversaries (Russia, China) trade concessions, narratives, and grey-zone moves across the High North — all below the threshold of open conflict.

1
Pick your role. Decision-Maker (breadth — act on the composite Cap Gap and Concession Engine) or Decision-Support (full depth toolkit — Cognitive Warfare, Narrative Analysis, Governance Lab, Capability Cards, Governance Analytics). Toggle it any time, top-left of the nav bar.
2
Set the scenario (optional). Tab 00, Scenario Input. Sharpens what the Agentic AI Red Team does each turn — skip it to use the default High North theatre.
3
Act. Trigger a concession (tab 03), run a Cognitive Warfare or Narrative analysis (tabs 04/06, Decision-Support only), or work the Contested Table when it opens.
4
Click "End Turn" in the header, any tab, any time. It advances the session and generates the adversary's next move under its active doctrine.
5
Watch the header. Turn, Cap Gap, active doctrine, and last AI move are live across every tab — that bar is the whole session state at a glance.

WHY THIS TAB EXISTS — Everything below comes from the GDELT Project, a free, unclassified, publicly documented open-source event database (no API key, no generative AI, no vendor endpoint). It's a direct substitute for the Anthropic API for teams that can't clear a commercial LLM call: real reported events instead of AI-generated ones.
